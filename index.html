Dublin City Council's register of vacant sites, a separate statutory register from the derelict sites. The vacant sites levy was replaced by the Residential Zoned Land Tax and the register has received no new entries since April 2022, though removals are still tracked; the Vacant / Idle (RZLT) view shows its living successor. Pick a site from the list or the map to see its ownership, valuation, and planning history.

Land in Dublin City that the council has determined to be vacant or idle under the Residential Zoned Land Tax, the successor to the vacant sites levy. These are the RZLT final-map parcels in mixed-use and regeneration zonings, where a vacancy determination is a condition of inclusion; blanket residential zonings, which carry no such determination, are not shown. Pick a parcel from the list or the map to see its zoning and history.
